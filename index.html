Alba Kalaja

PhD Candidate @ VSI Groningen

Hello visitor!
I am a fourth-year PhD candidate at the Van Swinderen Institute (Cosmic Frontier), in Groningen (Netherlands). I work with Daan Meerburg on primordial and secondary non-Gaussianity in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB). My research focuses on the higher-order statistics of primordial fluctuations, weak gravitational lensing, reconstruction of the CMB lensing bispectrum and cross-correlation with cosmic shear. I am member of the Simons Observatory collaboration. 
I am currently a Predoctoral Fellow at the Center for Computational Astrophysics (Flatiron Institute) working with Shirley Ho and William Coulton.
